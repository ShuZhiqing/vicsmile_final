Reciprocal Health Agreement


Reciprocal Health Agreement or in a plain language a mutual health agreement is a collection of agreements of Australia with 11 countries that covers the cost of medically necessary care when Australians visit certain countries and visitors from these countries visit Australia.

The agreement conditions tell you:
  • what medical care you can get under Medicare while you visit Australia, and
  • the documents you need to show us to prove that you are eligible under the RHCA
Australia has mutual health agreement with the following countries:
  • Belgium
  • Finland
  • Italy
  • Malta
  • Netherlands
  • New Zealand
  • Norway
  • Ireland
  • Slovenia
  • Sweden
  • United Kingdom
Click on the hyperlinks to read more information about specific condition of each country.
